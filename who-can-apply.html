Who Can Apply?

Accreditation is offered to organisations that provide testing, inspection and certification services.

PLEASE NOTE: Accreditation is not normally offered to organisations outside the United Kingdom.

How to Apply?

Please download the Application Forms and our Terms and Conditions of business, to apply for accreditation.

Cost of Accreditation

There is an administration fee which is dependent on the applicant organization. Each organization is different in size and expertise. When we receive an application, it will provide us with the necessary information to ascertain the fee for the applicant organization to become UKWWA accredited.
